## Why Your Posts Aren't Working

You're posting consistently. You've got decent content. But the likes, comments, and shares? Crickets.

Here are the five most common mistakes we see — and how to fix them.

## 1. Posting Without a Strategy

Random posting is not a strategy. Every post should serve a purpose: educate, entertain, inspire, or sell. Map your content to a monthly calendar with clear themes.

## 2. Ignoring Your Audience's Schedule

Posting at 3 AM when your audience is asleep? That's a wasted post. Use your platform analytics to find when your followers are most active.

## 3. Only Talking About Yourself

Nobody wants to follow a brand that only says "buy this." The 80/20 rule works: 80% value-driven content, 20% promotional.

## 4. Not Engaging Back

Social media is a two-way street. If someone comments on your post and you don't reply, you're telling the algorithm (and that person) you don't care.

## 5. Ignoring Video Content

Reels and short-form video dominate every platform in 2026. If you're only posting static images, you're leaving reach on the table.

## The Fix

Start with an audit of your current social presence. Identify what's working, cut what isn't, and build a content plan that's intentional and consistent.
